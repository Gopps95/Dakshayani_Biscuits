About Us

Welcome to Dakshayani Biscuits, where every crunchy bite tells a story of resilience, family bonds, and the triumph over life's challenges. Established by Sethumadhavan in honor of his late mother, Dakshayani Biscuits is not just a brand; it's a celebration of the indomitable spirit that transforms hurdles into triumphs.
Our journey began with a dream to create biscuits that not only tantalize taste buds but also mirror the flavors of life itself – sweet, savory, and everything in between. Sethumadhavan's determination to build Dakshayani Biscuits became a saga of navigating bureaucratic hurdles, overcoming corruption, and fostering unity within his own family.
At Dakshayani Biscuits, we believe in the power of family, the strength found in overcoming adversity, and the simple joy of sharing delightful moments. Our biscuits are a reflection of these values, crafted with care and commitment to deliver not just a snack but an experience that resonates with the spirit of perseverance.
As we continue to grow globally, our commitment remains unwavering – to bring you biscuits that are more than just a treat for your taste buds. Dakshayani Biscuits symbolize the triumph of love, determination, and the sweetness that life offers even in its most challenging moments.
Join us on this flavorful journey and bite into the taste of Dakshayani – where every biscuit is a chapter in a story of resilience and triumph!
